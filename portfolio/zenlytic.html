How Zenlytic increased rep selling time by 3x instead of admin time

real image coming eventually, probably

The problem

Reps manually wrote follow-up emails after every call and meeting — jumping between other calls, summarizing notes, and figuring out next steps by hand. What should have taken minutes stretched into hours.

The solution

Tied everything into one place: the call, the calendar, and the CRM. The system drafts the follow-up itself — reps just review and sign off.

The stack

  • GCal API
  • Recall
  • ElevenLabs
  • Grok

The outcome

Zenlytic increased rep productivity by 1.36x, measured by the number of clicks it took to close out a call.
