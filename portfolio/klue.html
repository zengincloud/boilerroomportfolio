How Klue optimized for responses and increased reply rate by 40%

real image coming eventually, probably

The problem

Prospects see a missed call and let it go to voicemail — then forget about it. 82% of reps don't leave voicemails at all, relying instead on a follow-up call or email, or just hoping the prospect calls back. Waiting on a callback that may never come isn't a strategy, it's a bet.

The solution

Built a workflow that gets inside the moment the call is missed — connected through Poke — firing off a text every single time instead of leaving the prospect to forget. Familiar, immediate, and impossible to let slip into voicemail.

The stack

  • Nooks
  • Poke
  • HubSpot
  • Grok 4.3

The outcome

Reply rate increased 46%, familiarity with prospects went up, and the messaging got bolder instead of hiding in a voicemail to be forgotten forever.
