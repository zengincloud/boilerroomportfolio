How to run a simple and automated enrichment workflow

The problem

Zenlytic was running way too many tools for a team its size. Reps manually pulled leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator and the Apollo Chrome extension, pushed them into Outreach sequences, and updated stale phone numbers by hand.

The solution

Cut Apollo entirely and replaced it with Clay.

The stack

  • Clay
  • Nooks

The outcome

Booked 300% more meetings than the previous quarter — 62 vs. 23 — with an easy increase in time saved every week.
